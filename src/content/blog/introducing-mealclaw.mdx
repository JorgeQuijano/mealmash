Let me paint you a picture. It's 6 PM. You're hungry. Your partner is hungry. The kids are hungry. Everyone is looking at you like you have the answer to a question nobody knows how to answer: what's for dinner tonight?

Sound familiar? Yeah, us too. That's why we built MealClaw.

## The Real Problem

My wife and I have two problems with dinner that sound simple but get annoying fast. First, we hate repeating dishes. Give it a few weeks of the same rotation and even your favorite pasta dish starts feeling like a punishment. Second, we genuinely cannot agree on what to cook. I'm fine with almost anything. She has opinions. Strong ones. So half the week goes by and we just eat cereal. Or order something. That's not a meal plan, that's surrender.

And then there's the fridge problem. You buy a bunch of groceries with grand ambitions, freeze half of it, forget about it, find it three months later looking like a science experiment. We were throwing away food and money because we had no idea what was actually in the house.

So we built something that solves all of this in one place.

## What MealClaw Actually Does for You

Instead of staring at the fridge hoping dinner reveals itself, you open MealClaw and it tells you what you can make right now. It knows what's in your pantry and fridge because you told it. It matches that against its recipe ideas and shows you what's possible without a grocery run. No more "I could've made that" three days after you threw the ingredients out.

If you do need to shop, it tells you exactly what's missing. Not a vague grocery list from memory, but the actual items you need based on what you planned to cook. You buy those, you go home, you cook. No waste, no forgetting the cumin.

And the shopping list updates itself. You change your meal plan, the list changes. You add something mid-week, it goes on the list. No rewriting, no starting over.

But here's the part we use the most: no more repeats. MealClaw tracks what you've cooked recently so you can actually eat through a varied rotation instead of defaulting to the same five recipes because they're the only ones you remember.

## The Decision Helper

Some nights you just cannot decide. That's fine. Spin the dinner wheel and whatever it lands on, you commit. No arguing, no negotiation, no "I don't know, what do you want?" back and forth until 8 PM. You spin, you cook, you eat. It's kind of liberating actually.

## It's Not Just a List App

This isn't a glorified notes app where you write down recipes and hope you check them. MealClaw connects your meal plan to your pantry to your shopping list so everything talks to each other. Plan a recipe, it tells you what's in your fridge and what you need to buy. Cook something, it updates your pantry. Shop, check it off, done.

## Try It

It's free to start. Head to mealclaw.com, tell it what you have in your fridge, and see what you can actually make tonight. No account needed to browse. Eventually you'll want one to save your pantry and plans, but the point is you can start using it right now and feel the difference.

We're not a big company. We're just two people who got tired of the dinner debate and food waste and decided to build the thing we actually wanted.
